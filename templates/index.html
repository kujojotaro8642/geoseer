methodology
How GeoSeer finds the unfindable
01

Define your region

Enter a region name or coordinates. GeoSeer works at any scale — from a 10km coastal zone to an entire geological province.

02

AI stacks the layers

DEM terrain, coastline proximity, NDVI vegetation, geology, drainage networks, and historical site density are weighted and combined.

03

Probability map generated

Every zone in your region gets a discovery probability score. Export as GeoJSON, PDF report, or QGIS-ready file for fieldwork.

Live case study
Konkan Coast, Maharashtra — Ratnagiri Peninsula

The first systematic GIS-based remote sensing survey of the Ratnagiri coastline. Five independent methods converged on 7 CRITICAL excavation candidates along an 8-10km Deccan Trap basalt exposure — including previously undocumented offshore intertidal platforms.
